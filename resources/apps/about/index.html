About Gosti

Gosti Client v0.2.0 Beta

Gosti is a free, open source, federated video game marketplace concept.

To follow its development, or to contribute, see our public repositories on our Github!

Gosti is built on:
  • Electron
  • React.js
  • Chia blockchain
  • WalletConnect v2
  • Python
  • libtorrent

Thank you for participating in the Beta for Gosti.
